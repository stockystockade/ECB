Corporate Advisory


Export Credit Bank (ECB) Advisors offers tailored, independent financial advisory services in Corporate Finance/M&A, fund raising and corporate strategy. Our clients include private and listed corporates, family-owned companies and financial investors. Whether the objective is a sale, capital raising, merging or an acquisition, we are committed to supporting our clients for the entirety of their financial journey and for the full life-cycle of their business.

Export Credit Bank (ECB) Advisors prioritizes providing clients with customized and independent advice, ensuring meticulous preparation, flawless execution, and efficient transaction closings while keeping clients' goals in mind.
The ECB Advisors team possesses in-depth knowledge of the mid-market segment and maintains a broad network across Europe, allowing them to navigate the complexities of this market effectively.
With over years of history, ECB Advisors leverages the vast and agile international network of the Export Credit Bank (ECB) Group, which spans 2 locations in Tanzania and MENA offices in 2 countries. This network grants privileged access to a diverse investor base, including investment funds and international corporations.
ECB Advisors boasts a team of highly experienced transaction professionals with a wealth of mid-market investment banking expertise. Their primary objective is to provide clients with superior financial advisory services and build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships.

Corporate Finance

  • Structuring and raising debt financing

  • Mergers
  • Advice on succession processes in family businesses
  • Joint ventures
